About Me

WEB DESIGNER / DEVELOPER

Hello! My name is Tracy and I am a freelance web designer, developer, and graphic designer. I have been developing and designing websites for many years, but I mostly did it as an enjoyable creative hobby and outlet.

I have done several freelance projects throughout the last several years for friends, family, organizations, and colleagues, and it has been a long-standing dream of mine to finally develop a portfolio of the work I have done like the one you are viewing today. I hope you enjoy the content I have produced, as I would love to create and develop any designs, websites, flyers, or anything else you may be looking for, for you!

Some of My Skills

And more to come...

Unique & Responsive layouts

Developing unique layout mockups and bringing it to life is one of my favorite parts of the process.

Using HTML, CSS, and Javascript, I can develop full-width responsive websites that work and look great on all screen sizes.

Personalized Graphics

These include event flyers and invitations, business cards, advertisements, brochures, and more.

I work through Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop to create these projects. I can work with images you provide or I can create them for you!

Content Management Systems

I have experience working on a variety of content management systems and platforms and have published on multiple.

These include Cascade, GitHub, WordPress, Dreamweaver. I have also worked on Wix and Weebly and can help you manage your sites.

Content Acessibility

I try to ensure that my creations are accessible, taking into account possible abilities or conditions users may have.

I try to follow WCAG 2.0 standards, which ensures that all my projects are perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust.
